Anatomical Coverage

The Chest-ImaGenome dataset provides dense grounding for key thoracic structures. We observe strong coverage across critical regions:

Lungs & Pleura
Heart & Mediastinum
Bones (Ribs/Spine)
Support Devices

Attribute Balance

Diverse Clinical Labels: The dataset maintains a balanced mix of "Normal", "Abnormal", and specific "Disease" labels.

Severity Indicators: Attributes include modifiers like "mild", "severe", "small", and "large" essential for longitudinal tracking.

Strategic Role

Encoder Training: These structured supervisions directly train the Scene Graph Encoder , enabling the model to ground visual features in specific anatomical regions rather than relying on global image vectors.
