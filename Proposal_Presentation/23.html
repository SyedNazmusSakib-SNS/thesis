Report Generation (Autoregressive)

Methodology | Slide 23

The Decoder Mechanism

A Transformer decoder generates the report token-by-token. It is conditioned on the fused multimodal context H (visual + graph + history) to maximize the probability: p(y t | y <t , H)

Candidate Sampling Strategy

Instead of generating a single deterministic report, we produce K candidates using beam search. This diversity ensures we capture potential variations in clinical interpretation for downstream verification.
