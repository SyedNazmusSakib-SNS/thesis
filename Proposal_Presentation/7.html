Research Objectives

Introduction | Slide 7

This thesis aims to bridge the gap between static image captioning and trustworthy clinical diagnostics through three core pillars.

Longitudinal Modeling

To develop a Differential Longitudinal Graph Transformer that explicitly models interval change over time, moving beyond single-study snapshots.

Structured Reasoning

To integrate Anatomical Scene Graphs to ground visual features in clinical entities, ensuring outputs respect medical anatomical logic.

Trustworthiness

To implement Verification-Guided Inference using Reinforcement Learning and Differential VQA to minimize hallucinations.
