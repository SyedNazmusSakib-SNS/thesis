Dataset Composition

Preliminary Results | Slide 27

MIMIC-CXR

The core dataset containing de-identified chest radiographs and associated free-text reports. Serves as the backbone for image-text pre-training.

Medical Diff-VQA

A specialized dataset of Question-Answer pairs focused on difference detection. Used to train the Verifier module.

Chest-ImaGenome

Provides scene graph annotations (objects, attributes, relations) for anatomical grounding and structured graph integration.

ReXErr

A dataset with expert-annotated errors. Essential for Preference Learning (DPO) to teach the model what not to generate.
