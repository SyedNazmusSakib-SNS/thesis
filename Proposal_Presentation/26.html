The Curriculum Strategy

Not all errors are equal in clinical reporting. The training process implements a curriculum that distinguishes between benign variations and critical mistakes.

Clinical Focus & Priority

Medically Significant Errors (High Weight)

e.g., Missing pneumonia, inventing a pleural effusion, confusing left/right lung.

Stylistic Errors (Low Weight)

e.g., Synonyms ("opacity" vs "shadow"), sentence structure variations.

Data Foundation

Severity rankings are derived directly from the ReXErr dataset, ensuring the model's penalties align with human radiologist preferences.
