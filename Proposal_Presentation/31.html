Role in Verification

This dataset serves a dual purpose: it trains the core reasoning module and fine-tunes the Differential VQA verifier.

High Overlap: Extensive coverage of multi-task annotations ensures robust generalization.

Fact-Checking: The VQA module acts as a critic, explicitly querying generated reports for inconsistencies (e.g., asking "Is the heart enlarged?" to verify a generated claim of cardiomegaly).
