Linguistic Variability

Preliminary Results | Slide 29

Report Length Diversity

Radiology reports exhibit significant variance in length, ranging from concise "Normal" findings to complex, multi-paragraph descriptions for ICU patients with multiple pathologies.

Ground Truth vs. Error Structure

Structural Discrepancies

Error-injected reports (used for preference learning) often display different token distributions compared to human-verified ground truth.

Training Implications

Models trained on uncurated data may drift towards verbose hallucinations or overly terse outputs.

System Robustness

Requirement

The generative model must be robust to length variability, ensuring it captures all findings without truncating critical information or padding with repetitive text.
