The Critical Void

The absence of longitudinally aware alignment and verification mechanisms is the key open challenge.

Current models excel at single-image captioning but fail to replicate the comparative workflow of real-world radiology.

The Proposed Solution Space

We need a unified model that jointly addresses:

1. Temporal Reasoning

Explicitly modeling interval changes rather than static snapshots.

2. Structured Clinical Representation

Grounding visual features in anatomical scene graphs.

3. Alignment & Verification

Optimizing for clinical preferences and fact-checking capabilities.
