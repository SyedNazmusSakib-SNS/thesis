Time-Aware Positional Encoding

Methodology | Slide 19

Why Time Matters?

The time interval ( Δt ) between exams fundamentally alters clinical interpretation.

Example: A nodule doubling in size over 10 days (aggressive) vs. 10 years (benign).

The Method

We inject sinusoidal embeddings representing the specific time delta directly into the transformer, allowing the model to "feel" the passage of time continuously rather than in discrete steps.
