Verification Mechanisms

Literature Review | Slide 14

Current State

Most RRG models are purely autoregressive (predicting the next word based on probability). They lack a dedicated "fact-checking" step to validate generated claims against the image.

The Opportunity

Integrating verification into the generation loop. By treating generation and verification as distinct steps, we can filter out hallucinations before they reach the final report.
