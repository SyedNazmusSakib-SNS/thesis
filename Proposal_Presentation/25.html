Multi-Objective Learning

We employ a composite loss function to balance linguistic fluency, structural correctness, and clinical truthfulness.

Loss Components

L ce

Cross-Entropy

Standard language modeling for fluency.

L t

Temporal Consistency

Penalizes inconsistency in Δ features.

L g

Graph Relation

Enforces valid anatomical connections.

L p

Direct Preference (DPO)

Learns to avoid errors in ReXErr dataset.

L v

QA Consistency

Aligns with Diff-VQA module answers.

Total Optimization Objective
L = L ce + λ t L temp
+ λ g L graph + λ p L dpo + λ v L vqa
