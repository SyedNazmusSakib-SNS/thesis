From Task-Specific Models to Generalist Medical AI

Introduction | Slide 2

Evolution of Medical Imaging AI

  • Early progress focused on task-specific models like classification and segmentation
  • These models solve isolated subtasks, not full clinical workflows
  • Clinical interpretation requires integration, reasoning, and communication

Limitations of Task-Specific Approaches

  • Classification predicts presence but lacks location, severity, and explanation
  • Segmentation localizes structures but conveys no clinical meaning
  • Neither supports holistic decision-making or longitudinal reasoning

Toward Generalist Medical AI

  • A generalist medical AI must detect, localize, and interpret jointly
  • It should reason over images, prior studies, and anatomy
  • Radiology Report Generation as the complete, challenging objective
