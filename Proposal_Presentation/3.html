Beyond Classification

Simple binary labels (e.g., "Pneumonia: 90%") lack the nuance required for clinical decision-making and treatment planning.

Structured Synthesis

Radiologists synthesize visual evidence, anatomical context, and reasoning into coherent, structured narratives.

Goal of RRG

To act as a capable "second reader" by generating fluent, accurate findings, thereby reducing workload and errors.
