Methodology | Slide 22

Input Modalities

V Image Embeddings (ViT)

G Graph Embeddings (GNN)

R p Prior Report (Text)

Gated Mechanism

A learnable gating network dynamically calculates importance weights (α, β, γ) . This allows the model to prioritize visual evidence when distinct, or rely on textual history when images are ambiguous.

[ α , β , γ ] = softmax ( W g · [ V ; G ; R p ] )
H = α ⊙ V + β ⊙ G + γ ⊙ R p
