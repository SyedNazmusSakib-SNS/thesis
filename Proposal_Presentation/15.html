Critical Analysis of Prior Work

Literature Review | Slide 15

Current radiology report generation models face three fundamental limitations that hinder clinical adoption:

01
Temporal Flaw

The Snapshot Assumption

Approximately 90% of existing models ignore prior exams, treating each study as an independent event.

Current: Cross-sectional only

Needed: Longitudinal Tracking

02
Semantic Gap

Unstructured Visual Inputs

Models process raw pixel data without "medical logic," missing the structured anatomical relationships required for reasoning.

Current: Raw Pixels

Needed: Anatomical Graphs

03
Optimization

Metric Misalignment

Optimization relies on text-overlap metrics (BLEU/ROUGE) rather than clinical factual correctness (e.g., F1-CheXpert).

Current: N-gram Matching

Needed: Clinical Verified Correctness
